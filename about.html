Our Vision


Vicinia

believes in strengthening relationships within local communities by allowing neighbors to help each other with groceries and general in-store purchases. When one finds oneself injured, sick, or simply busy with day-to-day life, a neighbor is willing to help, for a little cash of course.

Vicinia

brings an Uber-like service to the realm of online shopping.

Who is this for?


Vicinia

relies on cooperation from the entire neighborhood, so not only can anyone use it, but it's imperative that everyone does.

Vicinia

is especially vital to those who cannot easily leave the house, whether it be the disabled, elderly, or busy professionals. Anyone can benefit from having goods delivered to their doorstep by a friendly neighbor. Not being able to leave your house doesn't mean that you can't access your local stores because

Vicinia

has partnered with hundreds of small businesses to help strengthen the local community.

Make some extra cash!


If you're headed to the grocery store why not pick up some extra things so that Mrs. Delgado can bake cookies for her grandchildren? Just make sure you handle her ingrediants with love and care, the way you would with your own groceries, and you'll even make some money out of it!

Vicinia

wants to support everyone in the community, including those that are looking for a job. Work on your own time without a boss and you are gauranteed at least minimum wage for every successful package delivered.

Do not fear thy neighbor


For they are good! Meet the people that you never see from your community when they deliver you grocieries, toiletries, office supplies, and more. This is the underlying cause for our services, because we believe that people are good and that you do not needed to be a professional to deliver the simple things. Should anything go wrong, however,

Vicinia

offers a report button so that you can report your deliverer. We take safety very seriously which is why this system, along with the customer ratings system, is offered.
